Northern Ireland


This is the Northern Irish flag, also called the "Saint Patrick's Saltire"


The flag reprisents the banner of Saint Patrick.


The flag was first adopted in 1973 after their parliament was abolished.

Located on the north eastern tip of Ireland lies Northen Ireland, the founding of this country took place in 1922 when the mostly unionist population of the area wished to remain part of the United Kingdom.


Ever since this divergence of Ireland itself this has remained a controversial subject, this is mostly due to religion with Ireland being predominantly Catholic and Northern Ireland being predominantly Protestant.


Like the other countries within the UK (excluding England), Northern Ireland has a form of devolved goverment, this means that they follow the laws of the UK and it's policies and having a limited role in policy making.
